Design Engineering Project at Husqvarna

This project was carried out by a group of 5 people and was conducted in collaboration with Husqvarna Group, and the goal was to improve one of their green space products. By firstly conducting a market research we found that the ergonomics of one of their leaf blowers were problematic. Users of the product experienced pain in their shoulder after long usage. We improved the ergonomics by constructing an elastic harness that the tube can hang onto that distributes the forces of the leaf blower over the users body, and also designing a new handle that was more ergonomic and easier to use.
One of the major insights from this project was the importance of market research. When it comes to product development, it is essential to perform thorough market researching to pinpoint key issues that the users experience. To have a good understanding of the users and their needs allows for development of products that makes sense for them. Without this understandning, this can result in products that do not provide value and are therefore useless. By prioritizing market research, one is more likely to develop successful products.
